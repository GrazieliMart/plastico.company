Who we are?

Plastico was born from the idea of transforming discarded plastics in Ireland into something useful and sustainable. We are at the beginning of this journey, exploring ways to produce recycled plastic sheets from materials already processed by specialized companies.

Reuse & Repurposing

Encouraging customers to use our sheets multiple times before disposal.

Waste-Free Production

All offcuts and production waste are collected and reintegrated into new sheets.

Future Buy-Back Scheme

We plan to introduce a program where customers can return old Plastico sheets they no longer need, allowing us to process and transform them into new sheets.

SUSTAINABILITY REPORT

Our projects are aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals and reflect our philosophy of active social contribution through partnerships, work, innovation and values.
